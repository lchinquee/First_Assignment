Bio

Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi (AKA Kid Cudi) is a multitalented rapper, singer, songwritter, poet, producer and much more.
He was born in Cleveland, Ohio and his birthday is January 30, 1984.

Kid Cudi is widely recognized for his influence on contemporary hip hop and alternative acts.
